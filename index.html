You’re an ancient Egyptian God competing against the other gods to create the perfect landscape for yoursubjugated peoples. You and the gods adjust the landscape to best suit your people, building farms,rivers, cities, villages—and places of worship. The god with the best—most loved—landscape wins andclaims the whole land. Super Epic Tile Layer is a tile-placement game where players draw a random tile and place the piece tocontinue the beautiful landscape of the Nile and Nile delta. Each tile must seamlessly continue thelandscape while getting victory points by claiming important pieces of countryside and locations.
